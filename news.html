Freight market updates for drivers and partners.

Short, practical industry updates connected to safety, rates, fuel, and operating discipline.

Freight Market

Truckload conditions are tightening faster than expected.

C.H. Robinson's April 2026 freight update notes that truckload markets are tightening, with projected 2026 costs up 16-17% year over year. For KMG, this supports the value of disciplined dispatch, strong carrier operations, and clear lane planning.

Source: C.H. Robinson

Safety

Roadcheck season puts equipment readiness in the spotlight.

Arrive Logistics highlighted that CVSA Roadcheck Week begins May 12, with prior years sidelining thousands of vehicles and drivers. This is exactly why KMG emphasizes safety and service shop readiness before the truck reaches the scale house.

Source: Arrive Logistics

Fuel

Fuel costs remain a major operating pressure.

FTR's April market update reported diesel relief after weeks of increases, but costs remain elevated. That keeps fuel discipline, equipment standards, and smarter dispatch planning central to carrier performance.

Source: FTR

Rates

Spot and contract rates are moving closer together.

Trucking Dive reported that spot linehaul pricing climbed sharply from March 2025 to February 2026 while contract rates rose more modestly. A tighter spread suggests a freight market moving toward a new balance.

Source: Trucking Dive
